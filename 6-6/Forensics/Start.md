# Digital Forensics
 
Forensics is about finding evidence that is hidden, deleted, or not immediately obvious. What I like about it is that it is not just a CTF skill, it is genuinely used in real criminal investigations, corporate data breach analysis, and incident response every day. Every file you open, every photo you take, every drive you plug in leaves behind traces and forensics is the art of reading those traces.
